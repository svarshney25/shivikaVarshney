Our Mission

Code Her World is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that provides free coding classes to girls of all ages. We've organized Python, Java, and Web Development workshops along with Digital Holiday Card and App Design camps. The organization also offers a 1:1 mentorship program where students can receive personalized instruction programming. Volunteers can also start a chapter in their communities to host local workshops and camps!
